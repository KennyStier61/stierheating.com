What is AFUE?

AFUE, which stands for Annual Fuel Utilization Efficiency, rates how efficiently a furnace burns fuel. The higher the AFUE, the more efficient the system is. Carrier's most efficient furnace has an AFUE of up to 96.7%, saving you in your gas bill.

Blower Motor Types

Variable speed blowers are able to operate at partial capacity instead of only being off or blowing hot air at full blast like a fixed-speed blower. This not only makes your furnace more efficient, but also keeps your house more comfortable.
