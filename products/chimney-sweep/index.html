Chimney Sweep

If you have a chimney in your home, it is important to have it cleaned and inspected every year. There are over 20,000 chimney fires annually, but most are preventable. Dirty chimneys are caused by creosote, or unburned wood, that builds up on the walls of the chimney. If not cleaned regularly, this creosote can turn into a roaring fire that could damage your home.
